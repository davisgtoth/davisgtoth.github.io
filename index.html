~/about

> whoami

Hi! I'm Davis 👋, an Engineering Physics student at the University of British Columbia. At my core, I view engineering challenges as puzzles waiting to be solved 🧩. I thrive in environments where the path forward isn't immediately clear, using experimental design and first-principles thinking to navigate through ambiguity.

While I have a strong academic background in math and physics, my passion is applying these theoretical concepts to the real world 🌍. I love the hands-on process of bridging the gap between physics and hardware, whether that means designing custom electronics ⚡️, tuning control systems, or prototyping novel electromechanical devices.

Outside of engineering, I play Ultimate Frisbee! 🥏 I compete in the USA D1 college series for UBC, where I've captained the team for the last two years — recently leading the program to its first USA College Nationals qualification in seven years! I have also represented Canada 🇨🇦 internationally at the U20 and U24 levels, most recently bringing home a silver medal 🥈 from the World Championships. If you want to see the me in action, you can watch our crossover game against Japan at the U24 World Championships here!
